We Know Hawaii Business

Hawaii businesses face unique financial challenges: the General Excise Tax instead of a sales tax, high cost of living impacting payroll, seasonal tourism-driven revenue swings, and state-specific tax nuances. We've spent 12 years mastering these complexities so you don't have to.

From the hotel industry to food trucks, retail to professional services — Hale Bookkeeping understands how Hawaii businesses operate and what they need from their financial partner.
