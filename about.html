Our Story

Built for Hawaii Business Owners

Hale Bookkeeping & Tax was founded in 2012 by Alicia Nakamura, a CPA who left a large accounting firm to build something more personal — a financial services company that truly understood the Hawaii small business ecosystem. The name "Hale" (the Hawaiian word for home) reflects the welcoming, family-oriented approach she wanted to bring to financial services.

Hawaii's tax and business environment is genuinely different from the mainland. The General Excise Tax, Hawaii-specific payroll requirements, the seasonal nature of many island businesses — these aren't footnotes for us. They're the core of what we work with every day for over 350 Hawaii business clients.

Today we're a team of 6 accounting professionals — all Hawaii residents with deep roots in the community — serving businesses from solopreneurs to companies with 50+ employees.
